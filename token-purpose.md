# XDOL Digital Dollar — Token Purpose Statement

XDOL Digital Dollar (XDOL) is an Ethereum-based ERC-20 digital asset designed for decentralized liquidity, digital transactions, and blockchain ecosystem integration.

The XDOL ecosystem is structured to support decentralized finance (DeFi) infrastructure, liquidity operations, digital payments, and smart contract interoperability across blockchain-based applications.

XDOL is intended to function as a utility-oriented digital asset within decentralized ecosystems and does not represent equity, ownership rights, or participation in any company, entity, or investment structure.

The token is designed exclusively for ecosystem utility, decentralized transactions, liquidity infrastructure, and blockchain-based integrations, as defined by its public smart contract and official ecosystem documentation.
